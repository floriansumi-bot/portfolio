About

FS

I'm Florian Sumi — Swiss and American, based near Neuchâtel. By day I'm doing a Master's in Social Sciences (Media & Communication) at the University of Lausanne; the rest of the time I build software.

I'm largely self-taught, and I work the modern way — I build software by directing AI: designing, prototyping and shipping real applications with AI as a pair-programmer. The result isn't slideware — it's working products people can open and use, from a cyberpunk code-learning platform running real language engines in the browser, to a tool that turns a sentence into a finished Excel workbook.

Alongside the code I bring hands-on, people-facing experience — retail sales, cash handling and customer service — plus a teaching qualification (TEFL). I learn fast, I'm reliable, and I like turning a vague idea into something that ships.
